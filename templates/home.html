About

This project explores the Atlanta real estate market in Georgia. For this project, we gathered data captured by Atlanta PD and various historical crime rates, household income and median listing price data to help visualize what Atlanta not only has been through historically but also what Atlanta has gone through with the most recent 2019 housing market issues if any.
